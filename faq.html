Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about eligibility, documentation, approval timelines, and how our team supports you through the process.

How does the application process work?

Submit the Apply Now form, and our team will contact you to understand your requirement, collect documents, compare lender options, and support you until disbursal.

What documents are usually required?

KYC (PAN/Aadhaar), income proof, bank statements, and property/vehicle documents (where applicable). Exact requirements vary by lender and product.

How long does approval take?

Timelines depend on product and documentation. Many cases get an initial response quickly, and final sanction depends on verification and lender policy.

Are interest rates guaranteed?

No. Rates are set by lenders and depend on eligibility, credit profile, and lender policies. Any calculators or examples are indicative only.
